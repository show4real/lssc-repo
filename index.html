Steps to Add Money

Tap "Recharge"

- On the main menu, select the “Recharge” option

- This opens the recharge options for different currencies.

Choose BTC and Enter Amount

- From the list of available currencies (USDT, ETH, BTC), select “BTC”.

- Enter the amount to recharge (e.g., 1) and tap “Confirm”.

Copy the Wallet Address

- A BTC wallet address is displayed (e.g., 164w8RGB4y...).

- Tap “Copy” to copy the address.

- Tap “Confirm” to proceed.

Add Money ($100)

- On your external wallet or payment app, input the amount to add (e.g., $100).

- Tap “Add” to proceed.

Buy Bitcoin ($100)

- You are redirected to a purchase screen with Bitcoin selected.

- Confirm the $100 amount and tap “Confirm” to complete the BTC purchase

Send BTC to Wallet Address

- Paste the copied wallet address into your BTC wallet sender interface.

- Input the amount value and press “Send” to complete the recharge.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What is LSSC and how does it work?

LSSC stands for Lightning Shared Scooter Community. It combines micromobility services (like renting electric scooters) with a broader community model that may include benefits such as credit restoration tools, referral programs, and digital financial services. Users typically download the app, create an account, and can start using scooters or accessing services immediately.

How do I recharge my LSSC wallet?

You can recharge your LSSC wallet by selecting a cryptocurrency (like BTC), copying the wallet address provided, and sending the desired amount from your crypto wallet. Once the transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, your balance is updated in the app.

Is it safe to use cryptocurrency for transactions?

Yes, it’s generally safe as long as you ensure the wallet address is copied correctly and the network (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum) matches. Always double-check transaction details before sending, as crypto transfers are irreversible.

Can I build my credit score with LSSC?

Yes, some LSSC programs offer credit restoration support through structured financial tools or partnerships. Users may access services that report payments or activities to credit bureaus, helping them rebuild credit over time.

How do I get support if my wallet recharge doesn’t reflect?

If your recharge doesn’t show up, first check the blockchain transaction status. If confirmed and still not reflected in your wallet, contact LSSC support through the app or official support email with your transaction ID and wallet address.
